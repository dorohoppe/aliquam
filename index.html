Hi! My name is Dorothée Hoppe. I am a PhD student at the Center for Language and Cognition at University of Groningen in the Netherlands.

For my PhD project I am using computational error-driven learning models to explore language learning. I am interested in how we can explain seemingly complex phenomena in language learning with very simple, low-level learning principles.

Please feel free to contact me if you are interested in my work.
